# 10 Tasty Red Velvet Recipes to Try ...

I knew I was in love ever since that first bite of a **red velvet** cupcake and wanted to try more **red velvet recipes** to see if they were all as tasty and delicious as that one! The **red velvet** trend has taken over at grocery stores, restaurants, and bakeries every where, so lets join in with these **7 Tasty Red Velvet Recipes to Try **and see if we can bake a **red velvet** materpiece...

## 1. Red Velvet Crepes...

![Red Velvet Crepes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/redvelvetcrepes_495x312.jpg)

[Photo Credit](https://goo.gl/yqIy9f)

Okay truth is I've never had a crepe before, but what better way to try them for the first time than with a **red velvet recipe**! With a mascarpone cream filling and a chocolate ganache drizzle I don't see why I haven't tried crepes yet if there are tasty recipes like this one waiting to be made. Check out [Duhlicious](https://goo.gl/yqIy9f)for the complete recipe details.

## 2. Red Velvet Donuts...

![Red Velvet Donuts...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/redvelvetdonuts_499x333.jpg)

[Photo Credit](https://www.tasteandtellblog.com/red-velvet-week-baked-red-velvet-donuts/)

I bet homemade donuts baked with a **red velvet recipe** would be wonderful with a cup of tea or coffee on a cold day. My daughter loves donuts and asks to get some every time we go to the grocery store, if she sees this I'm sure she'll beg for me to buy a donut pan so we can make these! It looks like most **red velvet recipes** are a little red food coloring, some chocolate or cocoa powder and a few other simple ingredients. Follow to [Taste and Tell Blog](https://www.tasteandtellblog.com/red-velvet-week-baked-red-velvet-donuts/)for the recipe.

## 3. Red Velvet Pancakes...

![Red Velvet Pancakes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/Red-Velvet-Heart-Pancakes_510x335.jpg)

Photo Credit: [TidyMom](https://tidymom.net/)via [Recipegirl](https://www.recipegirl.com/red-velvet-pancakes-with-sweet-cream-cheese-topping/)

Look at these amazing **red velvet** pancakes, wouldn't it be nice to wake up on Valentine's Day to find your loved one in the kitchen wearing an apron and making these! Try your hand at them with heart shaped cookie cutters, some red food coloring, cinnamon, and a few other ingredients. Follow the link to [Recipegirl](https://www.recipegirl.com/red-velvet-pancakes-with-sweet-cream-cheese-topping/)for the complete details and find more **recipes** on there as well as on [TidyMom](https://tidymom.net/).

Macarons and **red velvet **together...what's not to love, oh wait they are heart shaped too! Make someone smile with this adorable **red velvet recipe, **how sweet would they be for kids to take into school for Valentine's Day or even to give as favors on your wedding day. Check out [Delectable Deliciousness](http://delectabledeliciousness.blogspot.com/2010/02/red-velvet-valentines-macarons.html)for the complete details.

## 5. Red Velvet Cookies...

## 6. Red Velvet Truffles

![Red Velvet Truffles](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/red-velvet-truffles-recipe-9_500x384.jpg)

## 7. Red Velvet Cake...

![Red Velvet Cake...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/cake_500x700.jpg)

## 8. Red Velvet Cheesecake...

## 9. Red Velvet Cake Pops...

Turn that cake into little **red velvet** cake pops for your kid's birthday party, a baby or bridal shower, or just for a fun snack to enjoy at home. This is a wonderful **red velvet recipe** to get the kids involved in, check out _Kids National Geographic_for more details.

## 10. Red Velvet Cupcakes...

![Red Velvet Cupcakes...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/red-velvet-cupcakes1_509x340.jpg)

[Photo Credit](https://www.gimmesomeoven.com/red-velvet-cupcakes-with-cream-cheese-frosting/)

This little sweet treat is where I found my inspiration to find more tasty **red velvet recipes **and who wouldn't love a cupcake, especially a **red velvet** one! Look for this recipe, that includes cream cheese frosting, and more over at [Gimme Some Oven](https://www.gimmesomeoven.com/red-velvet-cupcakes-with-cream-cheese-frosting/).

Whether you are baking for an event or just love to be in the kitchen, **red velvet recipes** are something everyone will enjoy. Have fun with food coloring and surprise the kids or a loved one with bright **red velvet** pancakes for breakfast. Or treat yourself to a tasty **red velvet** cookie for dessert!
