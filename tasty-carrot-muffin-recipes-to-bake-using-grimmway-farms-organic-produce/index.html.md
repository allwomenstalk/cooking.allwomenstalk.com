# 7 Tasty Carrot Muffin Recipes to Bake Using Grimmway Farms Produce ...

My daughters and I love **baking** and lately, especially around the holiday season, we've been baking cookies. But I wanted to try something new and have never baked **muffins** before so I thought I would give it a try using [Grimmway Farms](https://www.grimmway.com/)**organic carrots**. Find the **recipe** we used plus other delightful ones with these **7 Tasty Carrot Muffin Recipes to Bake using Grimmway Farms Organic Produce**...
Tasty Carrot Muffin Recipes Great for Any Season...

## 1. Carrot Cake Muffins...

![Carrot Cake Muffins...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/01/IMG_4922_426x320.jpg)

![Carrot Cake Muffins...](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/01/IMG_4951_427x321.jpg)

(Personal Photos)

When I was shopping for **carrots** I thought the [Grimmway Farms](https://theorganicreport.com/organic-produce-perspectives-talking-jeff-huckaby-cal-organicgrimmway-farms)Organic "MatchStick" carrots would be the best to use for their taste, freshness, and size. Though we still had to chop them into smaller pieces, it was easier than having to grate a whole carrot. We knew we wanted to add walnuts into the **carrot muffins**, but we weren't sure about raisins. I wanted to use them in the **recipe** however, my daughters didn't and they won! We really loved the way the **carrot cake muffins **turned out; the carrots were very tasty and made for a colorful muffin. [Moms](https://parenting.allwomenstalk.com/kids-lunch-ideas-your-child-will-love/), this is a great way to get your kids to eat their vegtables! Check out the recipe on [Cooks.com](https://goo.gl/G2gPuL) for the complete details.
