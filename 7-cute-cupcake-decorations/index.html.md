# 7 Cute Cupcake Decorations ...

Everyone loves a good cupcake. Not only are they fun to eat, but they are also fun to bake and decorate. I’m always on the lookout for cute and quirky decorations. You can really go wild with creativity when decorating cupcakes. Below are just a few decorations to get you started.

## 1. Wilton Make a Face Animal Icing Decorations

![Wilton Make a Face Animal Icing Decorations](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/kt/t6/tm1bfcgg54c8f4c389597075508141_400x142.jpg)

**Price:** $2.29 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Create a cupcake animal army with these cute face decorations. You can mix and match from an assortment of eyes, ears, and noses. Go wild and make your own kooky face arrangements. These would look great against a simple, creamy white icing.

## 2. Crate and Barrel Set of 50 Mini Cupcake Papers

_ **Price:** $3.95 at crateandbarrel.com_
These mini cupcake papers are perfect for housing bite-sized cupcakes. I love the colour combination of orange, purple, and green. These would be well suited for both children’s parties and more mature occasions. Your cupcakes will be nothing short of irresistible!

## 3. Wilton Hello Kitty Icing Decorations

![Wilton Hello Kitty Icing Decorations](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/e6/m5/dmnohlsl54c8f4c5785ec666888391_400x280.jpg)

**Price:** $2.49 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Go Hello Kitty crazy with these adorable decorations. Made from edible sugar, these Hello Kitty faces are a fun way to add some girlish charm to your cupcakes. They are certified Kosher and come in a pack of 12. Have some pink icing on hand to really take it up a notch.

## 4. Crate and Barrel Wedding Cupcake Decorating Kit

_ **Price:** $12.95 at crateandbarrel.com_
If you or someone you know is getting married, this cupcake decorating kit would be a fantastic idea. Cupcakes are a great way to distribute individual servings. This kit features romantic cupcake papers and sweet bride and groom cupcake toppers. These would be well suited for both weddings and rehearsal dinners.

## 5. Mini Gingerbread Man Icing Decorations

![Mini Gingerbread Man Icing Decorations](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/ro/k2/cxp41yx254c8f4c7bc42f665258916_400x384.jpg)

**Price:** $6.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These decorations are hands-down my favourite because they allow me to combine my love of both gingerbread men and cupcakes. These mini gingerbread figures are perfect for topping off your cupcakes. Plus they’re edible! Keep in theme and bake some ginger-flavoured cakes or top with ginger icing.

## 6. Wilton Purple Posies Icing Flowers

![Wilton Purple Posies Icing Flowers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fj/gy/wpgdmume54c8f4c8d8369540421529_400x423.jpg)

**Price:** $5.99 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Celebrate spring with these beautiful blossoms. They come in large and small sizes. I think they would be perfect for creating bouquets on your cupcakes. Decorate your cupcakes with fluffy icing and sprinkle these on for good measure!

## 7. EuroQuest Imports Deco Cupcake Wraps

![EuroQuest Imports Deco Cupcake Wraps](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/b5/t2/sa5f2k8m54c8f4c9ba3c4091975900_400x157.jpg)

**Price:** $11.18 at [amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These cupcake wraps are a decorative way to add colour to your cupcakes. I love the polka dot design. The scallop edging along the top is also a cute detail. The wraps fit most cupcake sizes and have an adjustable width locking mechanism. Have these on hand next time you bake up a storm!
