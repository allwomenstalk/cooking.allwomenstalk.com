## Setting the Perfect Table

Creating a stunning setting involves more than just plates and utensils; it’s about crafting an experience that engages all the senses. Begin with a floral table runner featuring prints that echo the imagery of the dinnerware. This creates a cohesive theme that is delightfully eye-catching.

Consider pairing the Garden Lattice dinnerware with sleek cutlery and crystal-clear glassware, priced individually starting at **$12**. The subtle gleam of polished silverware complements the gold detailing on the dinnerware effortlessly.
