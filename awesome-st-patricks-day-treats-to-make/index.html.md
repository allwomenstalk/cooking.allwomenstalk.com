# 10 Awesome St. Patricks Day Treats to Make ...

The St. Patrick's Day Treats that we have here will make that party you're planning off the hook. Most of the** fantastic St. Patrick's Day treats** we have here are for kids of all ages. But there are also some that should not be served to minors; these particular **great St. Patrick's Day treats** are for those who love Guiness and looking for the gold at the end of the rainbow in an inebriated state.

## 1. _Rainbow Pudding_

See top photo for reference. Aren't they delightful? This is one of the **awesome St. Patrick's Day treats** we have listed here that you can serve to kiddies. Click on the link for the recipe.

## 2. [Lucky Vanilla Cupcakes](https://www.sprinklebakes.com/2011/02/lucky-vanilla-cupcakes.html)

![Lucky Vanilla Cupcakes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/Lucky-Vanilla-Cupcakes-Sprinklebakes_500x372.jpg)

If you're skilled at baking, this is is one of the **St. Patrick's Day treats** that you must serve at your party. The ground pistachios look great on top of the frosting but the best part, in my opinion, is the horse shoe topping. Isn't it darling?

## 3. _Green Ice Cream Cupcakes_

![Green Ice Cream Cupcakes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/ice-cream-cupcake-1-550x366_500x333.jpg)

Ice cream cupcakes that are St. Patrick's Day-perfect? Serve me one! Or three, please. Don't worry, this particular recipe will not call for baking. In fact, this is so easy to make, your kids will want to help you with the assembly.

## 4. _Marshmallow Kabobs_

Basically, you will make marshmallows kabobs with rainbow and 4-leaf clover drawings. Drawings, you wonder? This is made possible with the use of edible markers. .That's right, EDIBLE MARKERS. Beyond awesome, yes?

## 5. [Beer Jello Shots](http://1finecookie.com/sweet/beer-jello-shots-guinness-for-saint-pattys-day-or-regular-beer-for-whenever-in-tiny-beer-mugs-with-tiny-foam-heads-omg/)

![Beer Jello Shots](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/afontIMG_5121-1024x683_500x333.jpg)

This particular** St. Patrick's Day recipe** will require Guiness. If you happen to be a fan of this beer, you will want to check out Jasmin's recipe. Now only is it funny, the photos are superb, too.

## 6. [St. Patrick's Day Pops](http://www.lindsayannbakes.com/2011/03/st-patricks-day-pops.html)

Do you like sweets on a stick? Yep, same here. And if you love making sweets on sticks as much as eating them, these are the** St. Patrick's Day treats** that you can make. Trust me, these will be popular at your upcoming party.

## 7. [Buttercream Dream Icing](https://www.mybakingaddiction.com/happy-st-patricks-day/)

![Buttercream Dream Icing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/St.-P-cupcakes_400x300.jpg)

Oh the pretty cupcakes you'll make with this recipe! I bet this frosting is incredibly yummy, too. If you want to impress your guests, this is one of the **St. Patrick's Day treats** you must make.

## 8. [St. Patrick's Day Cookies](https://amandascookin.com/st-patricks-day-cookies-rainbows)

From the blog: These cookies are uber colorful, super duper easy, and oh so fun to make! This is also a great recipe to make with your kids, so don’t be afraid to let them dive right in. Even if you are not hosting a party and you simply want to make something extra special for the holiday, these cookies got you covered.

## 9. [Leprechaun Lime Punch](https://pizzazzerie.com/holidays/happy-st-patricks-day/)

First of all, how awesome is that name? Secondly, how easy is this punch to whip up? For sure, this is the perfect refreshment for your upcoming St. Paddy's Day party.

## 10. _Pot of Gold_

Familiar with chocolate bowls? You know, the ones you make using balloon. You can easily modify them to fit St. Patrick's Day. Check out the link for a fun recipe.

Feeling lucky yet? If these **awesome St. Patrick's Day treats** don't help you host a successful bash, I don't know what will.
