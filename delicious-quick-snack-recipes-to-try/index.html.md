# 8 Easy Fast Snack Recipes: Quick Snack Ideas for Any Cravings

Finding **quick snack ideas** that are actually satisfying can be a challenge. Most of us default to grabbing a bag of chips or a few cookies when hunger strikes between meals. But these **5 minute snack recipes** (and some that take just a bit longer) are super easy to throw together at a moment's notice. Not only are these **easy fast snack recipes** more nutritious than processed options, but you can also make them in large batches to store for later. If you're looking for more inspiration, check out our [quick and easy breakfast ideas](https://cooking.allwomenstalk.com/quick-and-easy-breakfast-ideas/) to start your day right!

## 1. Cheesy Chex Mix

![Cheesy Chex Mix](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/1_cheesy-chex-mix_500x375.jpg)

If you love cheese, this quick snack recipe is for you! It's simple—you just toss 3 cups Chex cereal, 2 cups mini pretzels, and 1 cup cheese crackers with 3/4 cup grated parmesan, 1/2 stick melted butter, and a pinch of garlic powder together. You can find more inspiration on the [official Chex website](https://www.chex.com/recipes/cheesy-chex-mix/). Spread on a baking sheet and bake 15 minutes at 325 degrees F. The result is a crunchy, cheesy, yummy snack that you can munch away on!

## 2. Crunchy Chickpea Dish

![Crunchy Chickpea Dish](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/2_crunchy-chickpea-dish_500x500.jpg)

Are you looking for a quick recipe that is as yummy as it is healthy? Try these crunchy chickpeas! It's easy: cook a 15-ounce can drained chickpeas in an ovenproof skillet with 2 tablespoons olive oil and 1 teaspoon each cumin and smoked paprika for 2 minutes. Learning [ways to eat more protein](https://cooking.allwomenstalk.com/ways-to-eat-more-protein/) is easy with snacks like this. Season with salt, then bake 20 minutes at 425 degrees F. Then, take them out and enjoy!

## 3. Ritz Melts

![Ritz Melts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/3_ritz-melts_500x338.jpg)

Crackers are a staple, but [Ritz crackers](https://www.ritzcrackers.com/) take it to another level. This quick snack recipe is at the top of the list for good reason! Brush Ritz crackers with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, pepper, and dried rosemary. Top with sliced turkey and shredded cheddar. Bake for 5 minutes at 400 degrees F. Pair this treat with lemonade or water to fully enjoy the flavor!

## 4. Yogurt Treats

![Yogurt Treats](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/4_yogurt-treats_400x400.jpg)

This recipe works great for a hot summer day or when you're craving something cool and sweet. It’s a fantastic [healthy dessert alternative](https://cooking.allwomenstalk.com/healthy-dessert-alternatives/) to ice cream that still hits the spot and is very kid-friendly! All you do is place a scoop of frozen yogurt between graham cracker squares. Roll the edges in chocolate chips. Freeze until firm, then enjoy!

## 5. Sweet Cinnamon Chips

![Sweet Cinnamon Chips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/5_sweet-cinnamon-chips_500x428.jpg)

Even if you aren't usually a bagel person, this quick snack recipe might change your mind. It’s perfect if you love the flavor of cinnamon bread. Slice stale cinnamon-raisin bagels crosswise into thin rounds. Brush the tops with melted butter and sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Bake 10 minutes at 325 degrees F; flip, brush with more butter, sprinkle with more cinnamon sugar, and bake 10 more minutes. Now enjoy with a cold glass of milk!

## 6. Pepperoni Pizza Bread

![Pepperoni Pizza Bread](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/6_pepperoni-pizza-bread_500x313.jpg)

Here’s a snack recipe that works as an impromptu lunch or supper! Halve a small loaf of Italian bread horizontally and toast under the broiler. Brush with olive oil, then top with tomato sauce, grated mozzarella, parmesan, and sliced pepperoni. Broil until bubbly. Sprinkle with dried oregano. This is one snack that is always a hit and easy to make often!

## 7. Cracker Sandwiches

![Cracker Sandwiches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/7_cracker-sandwiches_500x363.jpg)

Here’s a unique quick snack recipe that’s definitely worth a try. Spread peanut butter on saltines. Top with thin strawberry slices. Drizzle with honey and top each with another saltine. If you're in the mood for something more savory, you might prefer the [Ritz Melts](#3) mentioned earlier. It’s a perfect mix of salty, sweet, crunchy, and tangy!

**Must-Have Pantry Staples for Quick Snacks:**
* Whole grain crackers (like Ritz or Saltines)
* Canned chickpeas
* Peanut butter and honey
* Frozen yogurt or Greek yogurt
* Various nuts and seeds

## 8. Fruit and Dip

![Fruit and Dip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/8_fruit-and-dip_500x334.jpg)

A classic fast recipe is fruit and dip. It's a favorite for many because it's so versatile and refreshing. Try it with pineapple, grapes, strawberries, cantaloupe, or bananas. You'll need to mix 1 cup low-fat sour cream and 2 tablespoons each brown sugar and lime juice, then sprinkle with cinnamon sugar. Check out [EatRight.org](https://www.eatright.org/) for more tips on incorporating fruit into your diet. Thread fruit onto skewers and serve with the dip!
