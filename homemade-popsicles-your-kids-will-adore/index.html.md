# 7 Homemade Popsicles Your Kids Will Adore ...

Homemade Popsicles are perfect for a hot summer day! No matter how old you are, you're never too old to enjoy a popsicle! And kids absolutely adore homemade popsicles, so you can't go wrong with any of these popsicles you can make in your own home, or should I say freezer! These are all my own recipes, which I have created and tweaked to perfection over time. I'm sure you will love them! Please keep reading for some delicious homemade popsicles your kids will adore!

## 1. Lemon Cream Pop

Here is popsicle you can make that is bound to be a crowd pleaser! Mix two cups of ice cream, one cup of marshmallow creme , and three teaspoons of lemon zest in your blender until well blended. Add 1/4 cup of milk, and continue mixing. Once the mixture is creamy, pour it into your popsicle mold and freeze until hardened! Enjoy!

## 2. Fresh Berries

One of the best things about summer is all the summer berries we get to enjoy! I love this homemade popsicle because it has berries in it. I've used fresh or frozen, and they both taste delicious! Mix one cup of milk with 1 can of evaporated milk and half a cup of cool whip. Add 1 cup of your favorite berries, and harden in the freezer. Now enjoy your treat!

## 3. Root Beer Float

One of my favorite homemade popsicles of all time, this one is a crowd pleaser for sure! Mix 2 cups of ice cream with 1 cup of root beer. Once everything is blended, pour the mixture into dixie paper cups and freeze until partially hardened. Place your popsicle sticks in and continue freezing until thoroughly hardened. When you are ready to devour your popsicle, simply peel away the cup and toss it out!

## 4. Banana and Nutella

What could be a more perfect combination than banana with chocolate? They fit together like puzzle pieces! This homemade popsicle will melt in your mouth, guaranteed! All you need to do, is throw three bananas and 1/2 cup of Nutella into your blender. Blend until smooth, then add 1/4 cup of milk. Continue blending until well mixed, then pour into freezer molds!

## 5. Chocolate Heaven

If you are a chocolate fanatic, then you will love this popsicle. And this is one of the homemade popsicles that kids will rave over! To make it, blend 3/4 cup of milk with 1/2 cup evaporated milk. Stir in one tablespoon of cocoa powder. Continue to blend until smooth, then pour into popsicle molds. Your chocolate treat will be ready soon!

## 6. Kool-Aid Pops

Here is a popsicle you can make that goes over really well with kids. I find that out of all the homemade popsicles I've ever made, this is one that kids love to help make as much as they love to eat! It's so simple. Choose your favorite flavor of kool-aid, mix according to package directions, pour into popsicle molds, and freeze!

## 7. Peppermint Twist

Here is a unique and fun twist on a homemade popsicle for a hot summer day! You can enjoy this cool treat while you relax on your front porch! Mix two cups of ice cream, one cup of milk, and 1 teaspoon of peppermint flavoring in a blender. You can add a dash of chocolate syrup if you like! This is one of my all time favorite homemade popsicles!

My mouth is watering just thinking about eating one of these delish Homemade Popsicles right now! You can make any of these popsicles in a popsicle mold, or you can pour them into paper cups. Either way, these popsicles you can make will turn out delightful and refreshing. Your kids are bound to be happy! Do you have any homemade popsicle recipes to share today?
