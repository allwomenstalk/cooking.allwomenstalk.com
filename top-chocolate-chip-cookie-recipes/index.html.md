# Top 10 Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipes ...

Ladies, get ready to drool, because I'm about to share the top 10 chocolate chip cookie recipes with you! Chocolate chip cookies. Mmmmmm! Mention those three words and you generally have every mouth in the room watering! And there's no denying that some recipes are better than others! I'm going to give you the top recipe variations! Your main ingredients are 1 cup softened butter, 1 cup white sugar, one cup packed brown sugar, 2 eggs, 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract, 3 cups all purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 2 teaspoons hot water, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 2 cups of chocolate chips. Cream the butter and sugars together, then add eggs. Next beat in the vanilla. Dissolve the baking soda in the hot water and add it to the batter. Stir in remaining ingredients. Now choose one of these awesome top 10 chocolate chip cookie recipes and follow the closing instructions to finish baking!

## 1. Add Walnuts

![Add Walnuts](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/5262332011_94a44ff973__2_500x375.jpg)

Nuts will add a nice crunchy texture to your cookies. And an unexpected flavor as well! I love nuts in my cookies. A word of caution-be careful when making cookies for others. Some people may have nut allergies, so it's best to ask first or warn people that there are nuts in the cookies!

## 2. Put on Icing

![Put on Icing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/99574310_6b2b2b1a5e_500x375.jpg)

Oooooh, what could make a warm, gooey cookie taste better? Putting on icing! You can choose between chocolate, vanilla, butterscotch, peanut butter and many other variations. You can make your own or used canned frosting. Either way it still turns out so delicious and yummy!

## 3. Mix in Pudding

![Mix in Pudding](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/51EWETWGXML___2_500x443.jpg)

This is one of the best top 10 chocolate chip cookie recipe variations ever! And it's great for cookies that you need to stay fresh for a long period of time, like if you are sending them in a package or making them ahead of time. You simply add a package of instant pudding mix in the flavor of your choice to your batter. You can then mix it in. Feel free to mix the pudding as directed first-go ahead and add the milk, then pour it into the batter. Either way will get you soft and fresh-for-days cookies!

## 4. Stir in Coconut Flakes

![Stir in Coconut Flakes](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/5765763956_912e1f856b_500x333.jpg)

I'm not a big fan of coconut, but I know there are plenty of people who are! Adding coconut flakes to your cookies will get a great reaction, especially from coconut lovers! Use as much or as little as you want, but the more you use, the more you will enhance the flavor and texture of your cookies!

## 5. Use Peanut Butter Chips

![Use Peanut Butter Chips](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/2615195166_63c615eba8__2_500x343.jpg)

One of my favorite top 10 chocolate chip cookie recipe variations is to add peanut butter chips or Reeses pieces to the dough. And you don't need to skip adding chocolate chips if you do this! No, just mix the two! Your flavor will be slightly like a Reeses Peanut Butter Cup! Now that's yummy!

## 6. Add Cocoa Powder

![Add Cocoa Powder](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/4171020748_95acb27cdf_500x332.jpg)

For an extra rich chocolate chip cookie, add a few pinches of cocoa powder to your mixture. It won't make your cookies sweeter, because cocoa powder is bitter. You can add extra sugar if you desire more sweetness. Cocoa powder will make for a stronger chocolate flavor and a richer cookie.

## 7. Experiment with Candy

![Experiment with Candy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/2422029839_3d9145ab0e_500x375.jpg)

You can find a top 10 chocolate chip cookie recipe that you love simply by adding candy to your cookies! It's very simple. You can choose between mini M&M's, Hershey's kisses, white chocolate chunks, soft mini-mints or anything else that strikes your fancy. In this case you would just swap them out for the chocolate chips. I would suggest avoiding hard candy so you don't present choking hazards.

## 8. Make Cookie Sandwiches

![Make Cookie Sandwiches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/5853466604_7ffaf6152f_500x334.jpg)

Everybody loves Oreos, right? Well, think like an Oreo and make a chocolate chip cookie sandwich! Just grab some icing, smear it between two cookies, and presto! You've done it! You can add sprinkles to the icing for some color and festivity if you would like.

## 9. Drizzle White Chocolate on Top

![Drizzle White Chocolate on Top](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/6a00d8341f663853ef00e54f4498138834-640wi__21_600x414.jpg)

Oh, this is my absolute favorite of the chocolate chip cookie recipes I've shared with you today. Melted white chocolate drizzled onto chocolate chip cookies fresh from the oven makes my mouth water! And it will yours too! You can buy white chocolate syrup in a bottle or make your own. Simply melt any form of white chocolate on the stove-top or in the microwave and drizzle it on the cookies!

## 10. Decide between Soft and Crunchy

![Decide between Soft and Crunchy](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/07/94414xcitefun-pnut-chocolate-chip__2_763x498.jpg)

Everybody likes their chocolate chip cookies differently. Maybe you like yours soft. Maybe you like them crunchy! Decide how you like your, then bake your cookies according to that. The longer you bake them, the crunchier they will be, so if you desire soft cookies, don't over-bake!

Now that you have decided which of the top 10 chocolate chip cookie recipe variations you would like to try, add the ingredient to your batter and coat a cookie sheet with non-stick cooking spray. Drop the batter onto the pan by spoonfuls and bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes. Now, simply enjoy! While you're waiting for them to bake, why not let me know about your favorite cookie recipes?
