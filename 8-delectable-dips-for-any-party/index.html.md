# 8 Delectable Dips for Any Party ...

There is hardly any doubt that dips are a must have for any casual party. They make perfect accompaniments to drinks and cocktails when combined with interesting dippers. Here are some extremely delectable dips for any party.

## 1. Hot Artichoke Dip

![Hot Artichoke Dip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/02/hot-artichoke-dip_500x335.jpg)

Image source: [lh5.ggpht.com](http://lh5.ggpht.com/_BizpeaUzxq8/TRF4ghwNzoI/AAAAAAAACjw/mrl06pkGw28/hot-artichoke-dip.jpg)

This delectable dip combines artichokes with mayonnaise, sour cream and a number of flavors like garlic, pimento and pepper. Garnish with Parmesan cheese. It tastes best with crusty sourdough French bread.

## 2. Buffalo Chicken Wings Dip

If you are a fan of Buffalo Chicken wings, then you are sure to consider this as one of the most delectable dips for any party. This dip combines all the flavors of your favorite dish. Best served with celery sticks and any kind of scoops.

## 3. Cucumber Dip (Tzatziki)

This is a great Greek dip that is cool and very refreshing. It combines cucumber and yogurt and some very interesting flavors. Serve with crusty bread, pita bread or any kind of vegetable.

## 4. Spicy Avocado Dip

![Spicy Avocado Dip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/02/guacamole_500x349.jpg)

Image source: [1.bp.blogspot.com](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/_Q3vn0T4nhaI/TE2xONWFXnI/AAAAAAAAAmo/4KktMazhPz0/s1600/guacamole.jpg)

Considered as one of the most delectable dips for any party, this is one of the simplest recipes ever. Just mash the avocado pulp, stir in grated onions, sour cream, hot chili sauce and salt and you are done. You can serve this with practically anything including tortilla chips, taquitos, nachos and so on.

## 5. Crab and Shrimp Dip

For those of you who love seafood, you are going to find yourself raving about this dip. Imagine two of the most favored items of seafood – crab and shrimps- in a dip. All you need is crab and shrimp meat, onions, chestnuts, mayonnaise and salt and pepper and combine all the ingredients. Serve with celery sticks, crackers or any other dipper you fancy.

## 6. Blue Cheese Dip

Lovers of blue cheese are going to think of this as one of the most delectable dips for any party. Blue Cheese has a strong flavor of its own, and when combined with cream cheese, sour cream, lemon and garlic, the result is to die for. Serve with any dipper you like; it will taste fantastic.

## 7. Smoked Salmon Dip

![Smoked Salmon Dip](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2011/02/smoked-salmon-dip-02_500x333.jpg)

Image source: [thumbs.ifood.tv](http://thumbs.ifood.tv/files/images/food/smoked-salmon-dip-02.jpg)

This is the kind of dip that your guests will ask you the recipe for. Although it requires quite a few ingredients, it is extremely easy to make. This dip also tastes great with any kind of dipper you choose.

## 8. Cream Cheese Fruit Dip

Take any fruit you like and make it into a cream cheese fruit dip. This recipe can be used with a wide range of fruits so that you can have a different dip at every party. Make puree of desired fruit and combine with cream cheese, sugar, sour cream and vanilla extract.

These delectable dips for any party will make your parties the talk of the town. Your guests will want the recipes of all the dips you ever present. The best thing, however, is that all these dips are extremely easy to make and take hardly any time or effort.
