# 7 Gratifyingly Easy & Delicious Crockpot Recipes ...

Easy Crockpot Receipes can be difficult to find if you aren't looking in the right place. I love using my crockpot for everything – especially in the winter. Throw on some soup, some chili or even some ribs, go to work and come home to dinner made! It took me a while to figure out what easy crockpot recipes worked and what didn't. Don't worry ladies – these easy crockpot recipes are fool-proof and delicious!

## 1. Cranberry Onion Pork Roast

at _tastycrockpotrecipes.net_
This little concoction is actually delicious. Just take a pork roast, some packaged onion soup, cranberry sauce and throw it all in the crockpot! How easy does that sound?

## 2. Chicken & Dumplings

at _tastycrockpotrecipes.net_
My mother-in-law makes an awesome chicken and dumplings – but she lives in NY and I live in MA. Since I can't get hers all of the time, I have to settle for this version of easy chicken and dumplings! I usually put this on in the morning and by the time I come home in the evening, the entire house smells delicious!

## 3. Beef & Gravy

at _tastycrockpotrecipes.net_
Beef in the crockpot is one of my favorite, but it's hard to find really good and easy crockpot recipes for beef. This one though? It's delicious and super easy to make. Just a few ingredients that you probably already have in your kitchen, some cubed beef and you have yourself an awesome meal when you get home from work!

## 4. Chunky Chili

at _tastycrockpotrecipes.net_
Remember how I said I liked to put chili in the crockpot? Well ladies, this is definitely one of the easy crockpot recipes you'll be able to throw on before work! It has everything that makes up a great chili in it and I promise – it's friggin' delicious!

## 5. Garlic & Lime Chicken

at _tastycrockpotrecipes.net_
I never realized that garlic and lime together make up an awesome chicken dish. Just throw some boneless skinless chicken breasts, some pepper, dried mustard, lime juice and soy sauce (among a few of the ingredients) into your crockpot and by the time you walk through your door in the evening, you'll have a mouthwatering dinner! This is totally one of the easy crockpot recipes you'll have to try and tell me if you liked!

## 6. Brown Sugar Chicken

at _tastycrockpotrecipes.net_
While garlic and lime make up a good chicken – brown sugar does too! This scrumptious little dish has brown sugar, lemon/lime soda, garlic and some black pepper – might seem weird but it's so good. Try it – seriously ladies, it's delicious!

## 7. Swiss Chicken

at _tastycrockpotrecipes.net_
If you're looking for something that hearty and rich to serve your family – this is it! This is one of the easy crockpot recipes that I love. It's got Swiss cheese, cream, mushroom soup – everything that makes up a hearty and great meal!

If you don't use your crockpot, now might be the time to start! These easy crockpot recipes are not only super easy to learn, but they are delicious too. What easy crockpot recipes do you have up your sleeves? Share 'em!
