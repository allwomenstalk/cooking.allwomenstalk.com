## 1. Slow Cooker Bacon Garlic Chicken Breast

![](https://img.allw.mn/thumbs/47/whqvxtjzplcxslzgbdwgt_236x354.jpg)

Four chicken breasts wrapped with thick cut bacon and placed into a crock pot in a single layer and ready for slow cooking.

This recipe is for slow cooker bacon garlic chicken breast. It includes ingredients such as chicken breasts, bacon, garlic, and chicken broth. The chicken breasts are placed in the slow cooker with the bacon and garlic, and then the chicken broth is added. The slow cooker is set on low and the chicken cooks for 6-8 hours.

To make the fajita seasoning, you will need smoked paprika, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
