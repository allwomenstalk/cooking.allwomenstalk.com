# 8 Healthy Pancakes for Breakfast ...

We all know that breakfast is the most important meal of the day. If you have a good, healthy and nutritious breakfast, it will give you lots of energy throughout the day. Pancakes have always been a very popular breakfast item. Here is a list of healthy pancakes for breakfast.

## 1. Baked Vegetable Pancakes

These are one of the top healthy pancakes for breakfast mainly because they are filled with the goodness of vegetables and they are also delicious. To make them even healthier, top them up with yogurt. You can also use apple sauce or honey as toppings.

## 2. Fruit Explosion Pancakes

These are also extremely popular because they are absolutely yummy as well as very healthy. Feel free to add any fresh fruit of your choice and top it up with yogurt with a drizzle of maple syrup.

## 3. Oat Banana Buttermilk Pancake

This is one of the top healthy pancakes for breakfast as well. Not only is it very rich in fiber, it is also very filling and will keep you sated for a long time. You won’t feel the pangs of hunger until it is lunchtime if you have a couple of these for breakfast.

## 4. Blueberry Pancakes

Blueberries are filled with antioxidants that help fight the negative effects of free radicals in the body and a great way to start your day. Make blueberry pancakes with flaxseed or whole wheat to make them healthier.

## 5. Egg Beaters Apple Oatmeal Pancakes

This is one of the top healthy pancakes for breakfast, especially if you are trying to lose weight. These pancakes are fat-free as well as full of nutrition. Many dieters swear by its effectiveness in losing weight.

## 6. Low-carb Pancakes

For people who want to consume lower levels of carbs in their diet, this is the best pancake for breakfast. You can make low-carb pancakes with bran flakes, cottage cheese, flax seeds, soy powder, wheat gluten and even pork rinds.

## 7. Peanut Butter Oatmeal Pancakes

There is no doubt that peanut butter is one of the mainstays of breakfast. A peanut butter pancake with a little bit of jam on top gives you the complete peanut butter and jelly experience in the form of a pancake and the oatmeal helps in making it a healthy meal.

## 8. Power-packed Multi-grain Pancakes

For those who want the goodness of grains in their diet, this is the most perfect breakfast pancake. Not only are these pancakes extremely nutritious, they are also very filling. And, it goes without saying that they are also very delicious.

These are some of the top healthy pancakes for breakfast. Not only will they provide you with rich dose of healthy food in the mornings, they will also give you loads of energy and vitality throughout the day. For those of you who want to lose weight, you will find that these pancakes are extremely filling and helpful in avoiding unnecessary consumption of food through the course of the day.
