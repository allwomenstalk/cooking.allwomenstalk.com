# 8 Colourful Kitchen Appliances ...

Kitchens are usually awash with stainless steel and ceramic white appliances. Why not add a dash of colour into the mix and invest in some bright and funky kitchen appliances? Think candy-coloured mixers and blenders in assorted stylish shades. The following appliances are only a taste of what’s out there.

![KitchenAid Artisan Stand Mixer in Pink](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/aa/a0/vcwf745154c8f2c608460033281800_500x441.jpg)

**Price:** $299.99 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
These KitchenAid stand mixers come in all [colours of the rainbow](https://lifestyle.allwomenstalk.com/colors-of-the-rainbow-and-what-they-symbolize/). It was so hard to choose just one colour to showcase! This sweet pastel pink one has to be my favourite. Make baking fun and add one of these to your kitchen!

## 2. Waring Pro Professional Drink Mixers

![Waring Pro Professional Drink Mixers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/k2/p6/z135yj5654c8f2c6f0705070350596_500x461.jpg)

**Price:** $149.99 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Mix up some milkshakes or smoothies with these colourful drink mixers. My pick is the apple green colour. This 2-speed mixer features an automatic start/stop button and spindle attachment. It comes with a mixing cup and stainless steel mixing bowl.

## 3. Café Retro Espressione Machine

![Café Retro Espressione Machine](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/wh/n1/p2mfzcol54c8f2c7e4d4d921687426_420x500.jpg)

**Price:** $399.95 at [crateandbarrel.com](https://www.crateandbarrel.com/kitchen-and-food/espresso-makers/cafe-retro-espressione-machine/s124242)
If you’re after a stylish addition to your kitchen, then this vivid red espresso machine is for you. I love the retro design. Its features include 18 bars of pressure, a removable water pump, and temperature gauge. This will definitely fuel your caffeine addiction!

## 4. Dualit Classic 4-Slice Toaster in Mint Green

![Dualit Classic 4-Slice Toaster in Mint Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/fc/og/t0x37g0s54c8f2c901398015268446_500x353.jpg)

**Price:** $319.99 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Wake up bright and early with this mint green toaster! I love the chunky retro design, as well as the combination of stainless steel and pastel matte finish. One of the many design features includes the energy efficient 2 or 4 slice toasting option. There’s just so much to love about this toaster!

## 5. Brita Pitcher in Violet

![Brita Pitcher in Violet](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/o7/xv/dxcftdxa54c8f2ca0458c685522384_500x496.jpg)

**Price:** $31.49 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Keep your water clean with one of these Brita pitchers. I love the vibrant violet colour of the lid. It has a 10 cup capacity and features an electronic change-filter indicator. This will definitely keep you fashionably hydrated.

## 6. Bodum Ibis Cordless Electric Water Kettle in Green

![Bodum Ibis Cordless Electric Water Kettle in Green](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/oc/gr/vfifubao54c8f2cbdc635697651753_500x404.jpg)

**Price:** $49.99 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
This bright green Bodum kettle is boiling hot! It’s made from plastic and stainless steel, with a glossy finish. The IBIS design features a stainless steel heating element which is integrated into the bottle of the kettle. It’s cutting edge in both form and function!

## 7. Waring Professional Blender

_ **Price:** $99.95 at crateandbarrel.com_
This professional blender is all sorts of stylish. It comes in bright red die-cast metal with a waterfall base and glass carafe. I love retro-style kitchen appliances, and this blender is no exception. What’s more, it comes with a recipe book so you can blend up a storm every single day!

## 8. KitchenAid Teakettle in Mustard

![KitchenAid Teakettle in Mustard](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/sr/hh/l350ailt54c8f2cdca8f3099718248_500x395.jpg)

**Price:** $19.49 at [target.com](http://www.target.com?ref=allwomenstalk.com)
Add a bit of sunshine into your day with this kettle. The mustard colour is what initially caught my eye. The rounded design is also quite fantastic. It’s made from carbon and stainless steel, with an enamel finish. It would make the perfect addition to any stove top!
