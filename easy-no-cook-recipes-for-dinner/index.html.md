# 10 Easy No\-Cook Recipes for Dinner ...

**No Cook Recipes** are ideal for Summer as.nobody really wants to heat up that oven or stove to rustle a meal. Actually there are several easy no cook recipes that will not drive up the heat in the house and will still be filling and nutritious. Now do not think that nutritious and uncooked means unappealing. In fact it is just the opposite; many recipes that require no cooking are very tasty and fulfilling as a main course. Here are 10 easy no cook recipes for dinner.

## 1. [Chopped Greek Salad with Chicken](https://www.eatingwell.com/recipe/249897/chopped-greek-salad-with-chicken/)

![Chopped Greek Salad with Chicken](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/1_chopped-greek-salad-with-chicken_500x334.jpg)

Here’s a salad that brings the Greek to the kitchen. This tasty salad is versatile enough to have any variation of veggies and hearty enough to have a second helping without busting any diet. The full recipe proves why this is one of the most delicious **no cook recipes** you'll find.

## 2. [Antipasti Chopped Salad](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/claire-robinson/antipasti-chopped-salad-recipe-1917917)

![Antipasti Chopped Salad](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/2_antipasti-chopped-salad_500x333.jpg)

Quick and easy with a not in the mood to cook attitude, this salad is a crowd pleaser. With only 5 ingredients, it an easy no cook recipe that can be fixed with minimal time and mess. This will serves 4 or 2 on a hearty diet.

## 3. _Chicken Pesto Croissant Sandwich_

![Chicken Pesto Croissant Sandwich](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/3_chicken-pesto-croissant-sandwich_400x400.jpg)

What could be simpler and easier than a delicious sandwich? Nothing! That is why this recipe and other **no cook recipes** like it are perfect for the family on the go and can be made ahead of time and grabbed from the fridge the next day. Mix with a side of vegetables and you have a full meal.

## 4. [Parmesan Tuna Sandwiches](https://www.thespruceeats.com/parmesan-tuna-sandwiches-recipe-482536)

![Parmesan Tuna Sandwiches](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/4_parmesan-tuna-sandwiches_374x560.jpg)

No more plain tuna sandwiches. This no cook recipe has a new twist on tuna. If tuna is not your favorite meat, you can use canned salmon or chicken too. Try this for a wonderful sandwich.

## 5. _Chicken Tomato Wrap_

![Chicken Tomato Wrap](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/5_chicken-tomato-wrap_500x336.jpg)

For those that just love the flavor of a wrap, this is it. Wraps make great **no cook recipes**, and this one's amazing. The prep time on this is only 10 minutes and can be stored for later use. No hassle here with this no cook recipe and any meat can be used.

## 6. [Cold Cucumber Soup](https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipe-ideas/recipes/a30307/cold-cucumber-soup-recipe/)

![Cold Cucumber Soup](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/6_cold-cucumber-soup_500x332.jpg)

Another easy 5 ingredient no cook recipe that is quick and satisfying. This cold soup is perfect for a hot day. Prepare ahead of time so it is chilled properly.

## 7. [Avocado-Lime Soup with Chipotle Chile](https://www.yahoo.com/lifestyle/tagged/health)

![Avocado-Lime Soup with Chipotle Chile](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/7_avocado-lime-soup-with-chipotle-chile_500x414.jpg)

Avocado may be one that you think you can’t eat because you will gain weight. You could not be more wrong. This tasty item is full of the good fat that makes you feel fuller than you really are. Some believe it to be a vegetable while others think it is a fruit. Just enjoy it and don’t worry about it. Lots of **no cook recipes** now incorporate avocado, but this is one of the best.

## 8. [Herb Spread & Beef Roll-up](https://goo.gl/u5OYQX)

![Herb Spread & Beef Roll-up](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/8_herb-spread-beef-roll-up_420x560.jpg)

This protein rich appetizer is great for the friends that are coming over at a planned time. The only reason for this is this has slices of cucumber in it and cucumber already peeled and cut does not store for long. One batch will make about 40 servings which are perfect for watching the day’s baseball game.

## 9. _Stuffed Sweet Piquante Peppers_

![Stuffed Sweet Piquante Peppers](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/9_stuffed-sweet-piquante-peppers_500x333.jpg)

After being at work all day or outside the last thing you want to do is slave over a stove to cook an appetizer for the party you are going to. That is why this is one of the most delicious **no cook recipes** for a perfect appetizer is a favorite for its flavor and quick preparation. You'll be ready to eat in no time, and you'll keep cool in the bargain.

## 10. [Crab, Corn and Tomato Salad with Lemon-Basil Dressing](https://www.myrecipes.com/recipe/crab-corn-tomato-salad-with-lemon-basil-dressing)

![Crab, Corn and Tomato Salad with Lemon-Basil Dressing](https://resize.allw.mn/1028x0/filters:format(webp)/filters:quality(70)/content/cooking/2012/02/10_crab-corn-and-tomato-salad-with-lemon-basil-dressing_500x333.jpg)

This eye appealing salad will fill you up with the summer’s most colorful vegetables. It is an easy no cook recipe, beautiful to look at and even better to eat. What are you waiting for?

Now it is time to put the 10 easy **no cook recipes** for dinner to the test and keep it as cool as possible in the summer. Push away from the stove and the oven and try some cool cutting boards and the refrigerator. You can even try making some of your own variations and let us know how else to make no cook recipes for dinner. In the meantime, what are your favorite no cook recipes?
